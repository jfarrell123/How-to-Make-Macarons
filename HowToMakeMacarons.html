How to Make Macarons

Baking the cookies

Ingredients

  • 3 egg whites
  • 1/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1 2/3 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 cup finely ground almonds
  • Food coloring (optional)

First Step

Line a baking sheet with a silicone baking mat

Second Step

Beat the egg whites in a bowl using a mixer or whisk until they are white and foamy. Now beat in the white sugar and continue beating until egg whites are glossy, fluffy, and hold soft peaks.

Third Step

Sift confectioners' sugar and ground almonds in a separate bowl and quickly fold the almond mixture into the egg whites, about 30 strokes.

Fourth Step

Spoon a small amount of batter into a plastic bag with a small corner cut off and pipe a small amount of batter to test. Make sure the batter is about 1 1/2 inches in diameter, onto a prepared baking sheet. If the batter that you just tested holds a peak instead of flattening immediately, gently fold the batter a few more times and retest.

Fifth Step

When the batter is mixed enough to flatten immediately into an even disk, spoon the rest of the batter into a pastry bag fitted with a plain round tip. Then, pipe the batter onto the prepared baking sheet in rounds, leaving a good amount of space between the disks that you just layed down.

Sixth Step

Let the piped cookies stand out at room temperature until they form a hard skin on top. This process should take about 1 hour.

Seventh Step

Preheat oven to 285 degrees F

Eighth Step

Bake the cookies until set but not browned. This process should take about 10 minutes. Then, let the cookies cool completely before filling.
